## Task
Define the first proving slice for the object-native memory migration.

## Goal
Pick the smallest implementation slice that provides genuine value without forcing the full architecture to land at once.

## Likely candidates
- checkpoint-first proving slice
- evidence-ref plus checkpoint proving slice
- receipt-first slice for side-effect safety

## The document must decide
- which slice lands first and why
- which crates/files in `mana` and `imp` it touches
- what stays as legacy/current behavior during the slice
- what verify gate would prove the slice works end-to-end
- what follow-on slice it unlocks next

## Files
- `docs/architecture/memory-first-proving-slice.md`

## Do not
- do not pick a slice that requires all claims/evidence/checkpoints/lessons/receipts at once
