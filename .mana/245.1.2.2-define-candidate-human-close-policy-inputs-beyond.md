## Task
Write a root architecture doc that defines candidate future human-close policy inputs and recommends a policy shape beyond the current feature boolean.

## Why this unit exists
Once blockers take over more early manual responsibility, the remaining human-close mechanism should be driven by better signals than `feature: true`.

## What the doc must specify
1. Candidate inputs to human-close policy, such as:
   - unresolved blockers
   - child completion/readiness
   - review state
   - evidence/verify quality
   - risky side effects
   - goal/feature scope and impact
2. Policy-shape comparison:
   - explicit policy/config
   - heuristic from unit state
   - layered model combining both
3. Compatibility story from current `feature: true`
4. Recommendation for the future policy surface
