## Task
Write a migration risk register for moving from unit-centric memory to object-centric file-native memory.

## Must cover
- duplicated truth during transition
- stale or contradictory materialized views
- historical unit/archive compatibility
- retrieval regressions and prompt bloat
- operator confusion around where truth lives
- concurrent-agent write/merge pressure
- overfitting the schema before proving slices
- imp/mana boundary drift during rollout

## Files
- `docs/architecture/memory-migration-risks.md`

## Why this unit exists
Because the transfer is only worth doing if the migration risk is explicitly controlled.
